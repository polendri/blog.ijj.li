About

My name is Paul, and I write code in the Great White North. I got my BMath at UWaterloo and since then I’ve been mostly doing full-stack web development. On the side, I spend a lot of time with Rust and functional programming.

As a carbon-based organic life form just like you, I stop coding sometimes to read, run, game, walk my dog, and fit in quality time with my very busy wife.
